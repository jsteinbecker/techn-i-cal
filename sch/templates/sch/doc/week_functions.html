Reslove Posioned Position

All the shift assignments are compared with each other, and the system determines which slots should be deleted based on these criteria:
1
The Shift is among the Currently Assigned Employees 'unfavorable' shifts.
2
Retrying the Solver Bot stands a reasonable chance of finding a new pattern that will boost the amount of 'favorable' shifts the employee will be assigned to.
After the operation processes, run the Solver Bot again.

Smart Sorts & Swaps

Looking at Employees already assigned to a workday, this function will attempt to maximize the employees assigned to their preferred shifts. Swaps are made by ensuring neither employee is moved from a shift they enjoy more to a shift they enjoy less.
NOTE: The behavior of this function is a work in progress. Based on its ording behavior, certain employees may objectively benefit more from the use of this function.

Fill Templated Slots

NOTE: Templated Slots will NOT be filled if the employee has a PTO Request for that day. Employees who work certain shifts are scheduled by a template (ex. 7P, OP, 3, N). These templated slots can be created before the bot goes to work by using this function. Bot success increases greatly if templating is filled in prior to its execution.
